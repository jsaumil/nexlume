THE RAMMKRISHNA GROUP

Our Story

Founded in 2022 by Mr. Sanjay Kr Aagarwal and Mr. Arav Agarwal, The Rammkrishna Group has rapidly emerged as a leader in premium lighting and electrical solutions in Ahmedabad, Gujarat.

Est. 2022

Started operations with Rammkrisshna Tradinng Company at Moneyplant Highstreet, Ahmedabad, offering premium electrical products to retail and design professionals.

Nexlume Born 2023

Recognizing the potential in architectural and indoor lighting, we launched Nexlume - our premium LED brand offering end-to-end lighting solutions.

Trusted Partners

Authorized dealers of L&T, Crompton, and Finolex, serving interior designers and providing comprehensive electrical consultancy services.
